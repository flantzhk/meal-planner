02 / 08

Week view

Tonight's hero carries a 96-pixel play button — the kitchen's first move is to press it.

Every meal tile in every strip carries a small play affordance, top-right of the photo. No hover needed.

Flexible composition

Tuesday's dinner — 2 mains + 1 side. Sunday's dinner — 2 soups + 2 salads, no main at all. Any role (main · side · soup · salad · starter · one-pot) can repeat any number of times, or not appear. The data model is an ordered array of {recipeId, role} — no fixed slots, no required components.

Empty state

Recipe slots without a YouTube link show a + Add video chip — the system requires one.

Today's cooking.

Stir-fry for lunch. Steamed fish with roast potatoes for dinner. Defrost chicken at bedtime for tomorrow.

12:00 PM · LUNCH · 30 MIN

Chicken & Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs · 600 g
  • Bok choy · 1 bunch
  • Garlic · 4 cloves, sliced
  • Ginger · 1 thumb, julienned
  • Soy sauce · 3 tbsp
  • Sesame oil · 1 tsp
  • Vegetable oil · 2 tbsp

Method

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce + sesame oil, 10 minutes.
  2. Heat wok very hot. Add vegetable oil. Garlic + ginger, 30 seconds.
  3. Bok choy, stir-fry 2 minutes. Push to side.
  4. Chicken in. Don't stir for 2 minutes — let it sear.
  5. Toss everything together. Splash more soy. Serve.
5:30 PM · DINNER SIDE · 70 MIN · OVEN 200°C

Spiced Indian Roast Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes, waxy · 1.2 kg
  • Garam masala · 2 tsp
  • Mustard seeds · 1 tsp
  • Turmeric · ½ tsp
  • Olive oil · 4 tbsp
  • Salt, pepper

Method

  1. Parboil potatoes 8 minutes in salted water — still firm.
  2. Heat oil in pan. Bloom mustard seeds 30 seconds.
  3. Toss hot potatoes in oil + spices.
  4. Onto tray, oven 200°C, 50 minutes. Turn halfway.
6:00 PM · DINNER MAIN · 25 MIN · OVEN-OVERLAP

Steamed Fish, Lemon Beurre Blanc

Ingredients

  • White fish fillets · 4
  • Lemons · 2
  • Butter, cold cubes · 120 g
  • Shallot · 1, fine
  • White wine, dry · 100 ml
  • Salt, white pepper

Method

  1. Pat fish dry, season. Steam basket over simmering water, 8–10 min.
  2. Reduce wine + shallot in saucepan to 2 tbsp.
  3. Off heat. Whisk in butter cube by cube. Don't break it.
  4. Lemon juice. Salt. Spoon over fish. Plate with potatoes.
⚠ BEFORE BED · 9:00 PM

Take chicken thighs out of the freezer.

For tomorrow's stuffed peppers. Place in a covered dish on the bottom shelf.

Touch targets

Every tappable element ≥ 44×44px. Play buttons enlarge to 24px on the smaller meal-tile thumbnails to stay reachable.

Vertical scroll

Tonight's hero is a sticky banner up top of the Week view. The rest collapses to single-line strips so 7 days fit on one screen without scrolling forever.

Bottom nav

Five tabs, full Inter labels, no truncation. Active tab gets the vermilion top border. Persistent across every screen.

Five design principles

Video first. Photography always. Shopping that knows its meal. Sharing without roles. Print for the kitchen wall.

01 · Video

Persistent play buttons at three sizes — 96px on heroes, 56px on timeline events, 32px on every meal tile and shopping row. Press play anywhere, land in one player.

02 · Photography

Every recipe carries an image. Tonight's dinner is a cinematic hero with ken-burns motion. The week reads like a contact sheet.

03 · Shopping

Each ingredient knows the meals it serves. By Meal default, By Store and By Recipe one click away. Source pills on every row.

04 · Sharing

One screen for everyone. Sharing is a permission level (Owner / Plan+cook / Cook only / View only), not a separate mode. Magic links for anyone — no account needed.

05 · Paper

Print today's plan, a single recipe, or the whole week — in a layout designed for actual paper. Ink-only typography, generous margins, hairline rules. Tape it to the kitchen wall and keep going.
